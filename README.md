# Weather Forecast App
### Allows you to get a 7-day weather forecast for any city by name

![Website Overview](./screenshotsForReadME/WebsiteOverview.png)

## Enter the name of the city you want to know the weather of in the search bar
![Searchbar](./screenshotsForReadME/SearchBar.png)

## The last 8 searched cites will be saved to the browser's Local Memory, and displayed below the search bar
*You can click on their names to search for them*
![History Buttons](./screenshotsForReadME/HistoryButtons.png)

## Today's forecast will be displayed in the prominent field to the right
![Today's Forecast](./screenshotsForReadME/WeatherToday.png)

## The Forecast for the following 5 days will be displayed in the blue boxes under today's forecast
![5-Day Forecast](./screenshotsForReadME/WeatherForecast.png)
